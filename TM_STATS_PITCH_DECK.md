# Slide 4 — How It Works

### Three steps from round to insight.

**Step 1 — Log the round**
The golfer enters their round data directly from the course. Either:
- Quick entry (score, fairways, greens, putts — 2 minutes)
- Full tracking (shot-by-shot with distance and lie — unlocks full Strokes Gained)

**Step 2 — The engine runs**
TM Stats calculates Strokes Gained across four categories — Off Tee, Approach, Around Green, Putting — comparing each shot against a calibrated baseline for the player's ability level.

**Step 3 — Personalised coaching**
An AI coach (in one of 7 coaching styles chosen by the player) delivers specific feedback on the round, names the biggest weakness, prescribes a practice plan, and tracks improvement over time.

# Slide 6 — Strokes Gained: The Differentiator

### The most important innovation in golf analytics — now available to club golfers.

Strokes Gained is not a gimmick. It is the framework used by every major Tour, every elite academy, and every serious golf analyst on the planet.

**What makes it powerful:**

Traditional stats tell you *what* happened.  
Strokes Gained tells you *how much it mattered*.

**Example:**  
Two golfers both miss 8 greens in a round. Traditional stats: identical GIR%. But if one golfer's misses are 5 feet off the green and the other's are 40 yards away — those are completely different problems. Strokes Gained shows this. Traditional GIR% does not.

**Why it's been inaccessible until now:**  
Building accurate SG baselines for amateur golfers requires large datasets and careful calibration. Tour baselines don't apply to 15-handicappers. TM Stats has built the first amateur-calibrated SG engine available as a consumer product.
